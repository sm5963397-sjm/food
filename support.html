Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes 25–45 minutes depending on your location and the restaurant's prep time. You can track your order in real-time from the My Orders page.
How do I track my order?
Go to My Orders page and enter your phone number. You'll see all your orders with live status updates.
What if I receive the wrong order?
We're sorry about that! Please call us immediately on +91 76667 45468 or email eatlosupport@gmail.com. We'll resolve it within 24 hours.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing. After that, the restaurant has already started preparing it. Contact support immediately for help.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method. Contact us via email or phone to initiate a refund.
